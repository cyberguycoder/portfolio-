Employee Management System

A modern web-based employee management platform designed to manage employee records, attendance, tasks, salary details, and performance reports efficiently.

About The Project

Employee Management System is developed to simplify employee handling and organizational workflow management.

The system allows administrators to manage employee information, assign tasks, track attendance, and monitor performance through a secure dashboard.

It helps organizations improve productivity, reduce manual paperwork, and maintain employee records digitally.

System Features

Employee Records

Manage employee information including departments, contact details, and positions.

Attendance Tracking

Monitor employee attendance and working hours efficiently.

Task Assignment

Assign tasks to employees and track progress in real-time.

Salary Management

Store salary details and payment records securely.

Admin Dashboard

Centralized dashboard for managing all employee activities.

Performance Reports

Generate employee performance and productivity reports.
